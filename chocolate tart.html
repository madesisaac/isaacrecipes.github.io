Chocolate Tart

Instructions
Pictures

Cocoa pate sucre

  • 240g flour
  • 20g cocoa powder
  • 125g sugar
  • 120g butter
  • 1 egg

Chocolate cremeux

  • 90g dark chocolate
  • 250g cream
  • 40g sugar
  • 60g egg yolk
  • 2.5g gelatine
  • 80g cherries (optional)

Cocoa glaze (3 pies)

  • 100g cream
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 140g sugar
  • 80g water
  • 6g gelatine

Cocoa pate sucre

  1. Mix dry ingredients together to incorporate cocoa powder
  2. Add and blend butter together until coarse like sand; can use stand mixer or food processor
  3. Add egg and mix until it just comes together
  4. Pour onto work surface
  5. Use hands to bring together into dough; add a touch of water via wet hands if needed
  6. Place between two sheets of parchment paper and roll until 3mm
  7. Line tart pans, use fork to make steam release holes
  8. Chill for 30 minutes
  9. Bake with beans for 15 minutes at 170C, remove beans and bake for 7 minutes or until sheen is gone

Chocolate cremeux

  1. Bloom gelatine in cold water, set aside
  2. Add cream to pot and heat until warm and starting to steam
  3. Mix sugar and eggs
  4. Slowly add hot cream to sugar mixture to temper eggs
  5. Once combined, add back to pot and stir constantly until temperature reaches 83 C. Do not heat too fast or stir too slowly, else you'll have a scrambled egg.
  6. Once at temp, add drained gelatine to the chocolate
  7. Pour hot cream mixture over chocolate and stir until combined and slightly cooled
  8. Add any mix-ins like cherries
  9. Cover until needed once tart shell is cool

Cocoa glaze (3 pies)

  1. Mix alll ingredients except gelatine together
  2. Bloom gelatine on the side
  3. Heat the mixture until cocoa is dissolved and starts to steam; no boiling
  4. Add gelatine and mix until combined and smooth
  5. Once slightly cooled, pour over the tart slowly and fill in cracks; let set before covering
